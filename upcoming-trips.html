June 25 – July 5

June 25 – July 5, 2026 · 10 Days

Greece — 7-Island Cruise

Get ready for an unforgettable Mediterranean journey as we set off on a 10-day adventure through the heart of Greece and beyond.

Our experience begins with a breathtaking day trip to Meteora — a UNESCO World Heritage site where centuries-old monasteries sit dramatically atop towering rock formations.

From there, we embark on an enchanting 7-night, 7-island cruise starting and ending in Athens, blending iconic destinations with immersive cultural experiences. This thoughtfully curated itinerary gives us a full day on each stop, allowing us to truly soak in the beauty, history, and charm of every location.

Destinations include:

  • The whitewashed magic of Santorini
  • The vibrant energy of Mykonos
  • The rich heritage of Heraklion in Crete
  • The hidden gem allure of Milos
  • The medieval charm of Rhodes
  • A cultural stop in Kusadasi, Turkey

With a group of 56 travelers already joining through Nirvana Travels, this journey promises not just stunning destinations, but a vibrant community of explorers to share it with.

From ancient history to sun-soaked beaches and unforgettable sunsets — this is Greece the way it's meant to be experienced.

July 5 – 7

July 5 – 7, 2026 · 3 Days

Istanbul, Turkey

Day 1

Arrival & Taksim Exploration

Welcome to Turkey! Upon arrival at Istanbul Airport at 12:00 PM, our representative will greet you with a personalized name sign and assist you with a private transfer to your hotel.

In the evening, meet at 5:00 PM in front of Moxy Istanbul Taksim for a guided walking tour through the lively heart of the city. Explore the iconic Taksim Square and stroll along Istiklal Street, known for its vibrant atmosphere, historic architecture, and bustling shops. After the tour, enjoy free time to explore at your own pace.

Day 2

Historic Istanbul Tour

After breakfast, depart at 8:30 AM for a full-day guided exploration of Istanbul — the only city in the world spanning two continents, known through history as Byzantium and Constantinople.

Highlights of the day:

  • Topkapi Palace — once the residence of Ottoman sultans
  • Hagia Sophia — a masterpiece of Byzantine architecture
  • Blue Mosque — famous for its stunning blue tiles
  • Hippodrome of Constantinople — site of historic races and political events
  • Grand Bazaar — one of the world's oldest and largest covered markets

Day 3

Bosphorus Cruise & Departure

After breakfast, check out and depart at 10:30 AM for your final day. Begin with a visit to the aromatic Spice Bazaar, then enjoy a scenic Bosphorus Strait cruise — an unforgettable boat journey between Europe and Asia, with views of waterfront palaces, historic fortresses, and picturesque fishing villages. Private transfer to the airport for your departure flight at 6:50 PM.
